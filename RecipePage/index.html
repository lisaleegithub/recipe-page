Simple and Delicious Spicy Vodka Pasta Recipe

I usually buy jarred pasta sauce when I make pasta because I think it is tasty enough. One day, I saw this spicy vodka pasta recipe by a celebrity which went viral so I decided to try and it was quite good! The original recipe was great but I added more butter, garlic, red pepper flakes, and parmesan cheese for extra flavor. You can also shrimp, chicken, or ground beef for added protein :)

Photo credit: fine cooking

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

  • 2 servings of pasta (I use rigatoni or shells)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, diced
  • 1/4 white onions, diced
  • 1/4 cup of tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon of vodka
  • 1 tablespoon of red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese (save some to sprinkle at the end)
  • basil, chopped, to serve
  • salt and pepper to taste

How to make:

  1. Heat a large pan with olive oil, garlic, and onion.
  2. Add tomato paste and cook until it appears darker in color.
  3. Add in the heavy cream and vodka.
  4. Add in the red pepper flakes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Remove sauce from the heat.
  6. Cook pasta separately. When the pasta is done, save 1/4 of the pasta water.
  7. Add butter to the sauce that you set aside. Stir over a medium heat until the butter has melted.
  8. Add pasta and the pasta water to the sauce.
  9. Add parmesan cheese and stir. Save some cheese to sprinkle later.
  10. Add more salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Top with a sprinkling of parmesan cheese and basil to serve.
  12. Voila! Enjoy your pasta!
